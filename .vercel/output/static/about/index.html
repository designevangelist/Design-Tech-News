👋 About Us

Built for techies who deserve better access to opportunity

DesignTechNews is a curated platform that helps designers, developers, and digital creators discover opportunities, jobs, and industry news across Africa and the global tech ecosystem.

🎯 Our Mission

Making opportunities accessible

The goal is simple — make it easier for talented people to find fellowships, internships, accelerators, tech jobs, and important updates shaping the future of design and technology.

Too many brilliant designers and developers across Africa and beyond never reach their full potential — not because of lack of talent, but because they simply don't know what's available to them. DesignTechNews exists to close that gap.

Lebari Pado

Founder & Curator, DesignTechNews

DesignTechNews was born out of personal frustration. Early in my career as a designer, I spent countless hours scattered across dozens of websites, social media feeds, and WhatsApp groups just trying to find opportunities that were relevant to me — scholarships I qualified for, jobs that matched my skills, fellowships that could open doors. The information existed, but it was fragmented, buried, and hard to trust.

I built DesignTechNews to be the resource I wished I had — a single, reliable place where creatives across Africa and the world can find what they need without the noise. Every opportunity, job listing, and article is hand-picked with the community in mind.

DesignTechNews is an independent project built and curated by a designer and tech enthusiast passionate about connecting creatives with opportunities that can genuinely change their lives.
