Accelerator 🌍 Africa (Hybrid)

Google for Startups Accelerator Africa 2026

A three-month equity-free hybrid accelerator for growth-stage African tech startups, offering Google mentorship, tools, and up to $350,000 in Cloud credits.

Type

Accelerator

Deadline

March 18, 2026

Location

Africa (Hybrid)

The Google for Startups Accelerator Africa 2026 is now open for applications. This is the 10th cohort of Google’s flagship African accelerator, designed for growth-stage, AI-driven startups across the continent.

What You Get

  • Equity-free support — you keep full ownership of your company
  • Up to $350,000 in Google Cloud credits
  • Direct mentorship from Google engineers and product experts
  • Access to Google’s global startup network and investor community
  • Deep dives on product design, customer acquisition, and leadership

Program Structure

Each cohort includes 10–15 startups. The three-month hybrid program combines remote mentorship sessions, in-person sprints, 1-to-1 expert pairing, and group workshops.

Eligibility

  • Technology startup headquartered in Africa
  • Growth-stage (post-revenue or strong traction)
  • CTO or technical co-founder must commit to full program participation
  • Building a scalable, tech-driven solution to an African or global challenge

How to Apply

Apply through the Google for Startups portal before March 18, 2026.
