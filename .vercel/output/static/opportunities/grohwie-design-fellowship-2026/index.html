Grohwie Design Fellowship 2026

A fellowship for emerging designers to develop their skills and build impactful products.

Type

Fellowship

Deadline

2025-09-30

Location

Remote

The Grohwie Design Fellowship 2026 is now open for applications. The program is designed for early-career digital designers who want to develop their creative thinking, collaboration skills, and professional network.

Who should apply

  • Early-career digital designers
  • UI/UX, product, and visual designers
  • Designers interested in expanding their creative and leadership skills

What fellows gain

  • Mentorship from industry professionals
  • Training in design thinking and innovation
  • Access to a global community of designers
  • Career growth opportunities
