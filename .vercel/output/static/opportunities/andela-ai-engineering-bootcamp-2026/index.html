Andela AI Engineering Bootcamp 2026

A free, intensive AI engineering bootcamp by Andela designed to equip African developers with the skills to build and deploy AI-powered products.

Type

Fellowship

Deadline

February 11, 2026

Location

Remote – Africa

Andela has opened applications for its 2026 AI Engineering Bootcamp — a fully free, intensive program designed to equip African developers with cutting-edge AI engineering competencies and connect them to global tech opportunities.

What You Get

  • Free access to a structured AI engineering curriculum
  • Hands-on projects building real AI-powered applications
  • Mentorship from Andela engineers and industry professionals
  • Job placement support and connection to Andela’s global employer network
  • Certificate of completion recognized by leading tech companies

What You’ll Learn

  • Machine learning fundamentals and model training
  • Building and deploying AI APIs and pipelines
  • Working with LLMs and prompt engineering
  • AI product development and MLOps basics

Eligibility

  • African developer with foundational programming skills
  • Ability to commit to the full bootcamp schedule
  • No prior AI experience required — strong motivation to learn is key

How to Apply

Apply through the Andela website before February 11, 2026. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.
