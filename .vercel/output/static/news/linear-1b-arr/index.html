Linear Hits $1B ARR and Announces Major Enterprise Expansion

The beloved project management tool for engineers crosses a key revenue milestone and launches enterprise features targeting Fortune 500 companies.

Linear, the project management tool beloved by engineering teams worldwide, has announced it has crossed $1 billion in annual recurring revenue — a milestone that cements its place among the most successful B2B SaaS products of its generation.

The Growth Story

Linear launched in 2020 with a clear thesis: build the fastest, most opinionated project management tool for software teams. By refusing to bloat the product with endless features, the team cultivated an intensely loyal user base that grew primarily through word of mouth.

Enterprise Push

With the ARR milestone, Linear is now targeting Fortune 500 companies with a new Enterprise tier that includes SSO, advanced permissions, compliance tooling, and dedicated support.

What Makes Linear Different

Linear’s success proves that in a crowded market, opinionated products with exceptional UX can win against incumbents like Jira. Speed and simplicity remain the product’s core competitive advantages.
