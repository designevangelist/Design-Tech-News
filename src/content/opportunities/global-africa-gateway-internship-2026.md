The Africa Center and Afreximbank have opened applications for the 2026 Global Africa Gateway Summer Internship Program. This initiative matches highly skilled candidates based in the U.S. with leading companies and organizations across the African continent.

## What You Get

- $10,000 stipend covering travel, lodging, and expenses
- Substantive professional experience with a reputable African organization
- Access to the Africa Center's global network of leaders and investors
- Host organizations include Afreximbank and ARISE Integrated Industrial Platforms

## Available Tracks

Finance, business analytics, investment banking, construction, commodities, law, and NGO management.

## Eligibility

- U.S. citizens of African descent
- Currently enrolled in full-time graduate study or recently graduated (within 6 months)
- Aged between 22 and 35 at time of application
- Fluent in English and at least one other AU official language (Arabic, French, Portuguese, or Kiswahili)

## How to Apply

Submit a 1,000-word personal statement, CV, and valid passport to **careers@theafricacenter.org** before **March 6, 2026**.
