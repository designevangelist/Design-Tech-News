The Mastercard Foundation EdTech Fellowship is now open for its 2025 cohort across multiple African countries, implemented through leading innovation hubs including CcHUB (Nigeria), iHub (Kenya), Injini (South Africa), MEST Africa (Ghana), and Sahara Consult (Tanzania).

## What You Get

- Business development and financial management training
- Access to the science of learning and EdTech-specific mentorship
- Connections to investors and education sector stakeholders
- Demo Day showcase in your country
- Network of 90+ EdTech alumni companies across Africa

## Who Should Apply

African EdTech startups that are early to growth stage, building solutions that make education more accessible, inclusive, and affordable through technology.

## Eligibility

- EdTech startup based in one of the eligible African countries
- Early to growth stage with a working product
- Founder must be able to commit to the full fellowship program

## How to Apply

Applications are managed through each implementing partner hub. Visit the Mastercard Foundation website and select the hub in your country to apply.
