She Code Africa has opened applications for the Q4 2025 Laptop Scholarship, proudly sponsored by HP. This initiative bridges the digital divide for African women and girls passionate about building careers in technology.

## What You Get

- A brand-new HP laptop
- Access to the She Code Africa community of 70,000+ women in tech
- Mentorship and learning resources to accelerate your tech career

## Who Should Apply

This scholarship is open to **all African women and girls** — not just She Code Africa members. If you are passionate about technology but face barriers accessing a laptop, this is for you.

## Eligibility

- African woman or girl interested in or currently pursuing a tech career
- Does not currently have access to a personal laptop
- Can clearly demonstrate how a laptop will support your tech goals

## How to Apply

Complete the online application form honestly, describing your aspirations and current challenges. Apply before **October 28, 2025**.
