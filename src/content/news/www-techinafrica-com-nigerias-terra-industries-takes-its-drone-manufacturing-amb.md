Africas most-funded defence technology startup is building its biggest factory yet — and its not in Nigeria. Terra Industries has announced the construction of a 34,000-square-foot drone manufacturing facility in Accra, Ghana, named Pax-2, which will serve as the companys primary regional manufacturing base for drone and counter-drone systems.
