Jonathan Smit, the founder and former Managing Director of Payfast, has announced his acquisition of iVeri, one of Africa’s longest-standing payment technology providers. The move marks a significant shift in the continents fintech landscape, bringing together iVeris nearly 30-year legacy of robust payment infrastructure and the vision of one of South Africas most accomplished entrepreneurs.
