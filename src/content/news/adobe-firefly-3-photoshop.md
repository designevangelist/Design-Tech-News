Adobe has released Firefly 3, a major upgrade to its generative AI model that introduces the ability to understand and edit layered Photoshop files through natural language instructions.

## What's New in Firefly 3

Unlike previous versions that generated standalone images, Firefly 3 can parse the full layer structure of a PSD file and make precise, context-aware edits — changing colors on specific layers, swapping elements, adjusting lighting, and more.

## Real-World Use Cases

- Rebranding campaigns: update colors and logos across hundreds of assets in one prompt
- Photo retouching: describe the edit in plain English instead of manual masking
- Layout variations: generate multiple versions of a design for A/B testing

## Availability

Firefly 3 is available now to all Adobe Creative Cloud subscribers through the Firefly web app and directly within Photoshop beta.
