Cascador, the Nigeria-based ScaleUp Program supporting growth-stage, mission-driven founders building scalable businesses with meaningful social and economic impact, has appointed Oyin Solebo as its Chief Operating Officer (COO). Her appointment coincides with the programs strategic shift to streamline its platform for scaling high-impact growth-stage companies and precedes its 2026 ScaleUp cycle.
