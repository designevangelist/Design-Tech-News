Wave, the Dakar-based mobile money platform, has closed a $137 million Series B round led by Stripe and Sequoia Capital, with participation from several pan-African funds.

## About Wave

Wave operates one of the lowest-fee mobile money services in Africa, charging just 1% per transaction compared to the industry average of 5–7%. The company currently serves over 8 million users across Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Mali, and Uganda.

## Expansion Plans

The fresh capital will fund expansion into five new francophone African markets over the next 18 months, along with investments in merchant payment infrastructure and agent network growth.

## Why It Matters

Wave's model challenges the dominance of telecoms-owned mobile money services and demonstrates that fintech built specifically for African users — not adapted from Western models — can scale rapidly and profitably.
