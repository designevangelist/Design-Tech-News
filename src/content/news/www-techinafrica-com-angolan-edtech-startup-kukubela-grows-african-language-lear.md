Angolan startup Kukubela is expanding its presence in the African edtech market by offering digital courses in local and regional languages such as Kimbundu, Kikongo, Umbundu, Tshokwé and Lingala. Founded in Luanda in 2023 by António Nicolau, the platform combines language instruction with cultural content designed by native speakers, aiming to preserve African linguistic heritage [] The post Ango
