Moniepoint, the Lagos-based business banking and payments platform, has officially crossed the $1 billion valuation mark, becoming Africa's newest unicorn.

## About Moniepoint

Founded in 2015, Moniepoint provides business accounts, payment terminals, credit, and expense management tools to small and medium businesses across Nigeria. The platform has grown to serve over 10 million businesses and processes more than $182 billion in transactions annually.

## The Funding Round

The unicorn milestone came after a $110 million Series C extension led by Development Partners International and Google's Africa Investment Fund, bringing total funding to over $250 million.

## Significance for Africa

Moniepoint's growth reflects the massive opportunity in serving Africa's underbanked business owners with purpose-built financial infrastructure rather than adapting products designed for Western markets.
