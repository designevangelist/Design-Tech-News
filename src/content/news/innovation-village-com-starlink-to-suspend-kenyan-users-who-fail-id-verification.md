Starlink has started suspending users in Kenya who failed to comply with a mandatory identity verification requirement, following the expiration of an April 30, 2026 deadline set for registration compliance. Affected customers began receiving suspension notices shortly after the deadline passed, confirming that access to the service had been temporarily blocked until required identity information
