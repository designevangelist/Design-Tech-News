Accelerate36, a collaborative initiative aimed at strengthening Namibia’s startup ecosystem and create a pathway for high-growth startups to access public capital markets, has been launched in Windhoek. A multi-partner initiative, Accelerate36 aims to help Namibia unlock economic growth by developing a pipeline of scalable startups and enabling their listing on the Development Capital Board (DevX)
