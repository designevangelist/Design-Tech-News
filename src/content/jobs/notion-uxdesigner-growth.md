Notion's Growth team is looking for a UI/UX Designer to help drive activation and retention through thoughtful, data-informed design across our web and mobile products.

## Responsibilities

- Design and iterate on onboarding flows and growth experiments
- Work closely with growth engineers and PMs on A/B tests
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups
- Analyze user behavior data to identify design opportunities

## Requirements

- 3+ years of UI/UX design experience
- Strong portfolio with growth or conversion-focused work
- Proficiency in Figma
- Comfort working in a fast-paced, experiment-driven environment
