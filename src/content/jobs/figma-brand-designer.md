Figma is looking for a Brand Designer to help shape how the world sees Figma. You'll craft visual identities, campaign assets, and brand experiences that inspire millions of designers globally.

## Responsibilities

- Design brand campaigns, marketing assets, and event visuals
- Evolve and maintain Figma's visual identity system
- Collaborate with marketing, content, and product teams
- Create motion graphics and animated brand assets

## Requirements

- 4+ years of brand or graphic design experience
- Strong portfolio showing conceptual and visual craft
- Proficiency in Figma, Illustrator, and After Effects
- Passion for design tools and the design community
