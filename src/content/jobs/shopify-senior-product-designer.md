Shopify is looking for a Senior Product Designer to help shape the future of commerce. You'll work on core merchant-facing products used by millions of businesses worldwide.

## Responsibilities

- Lead design for key product areas from concept to launch
- Collaborate with product managers and engineers to deliver polished experiences
- Conduct user research and translate insights into design decisions
- Contribute to and evolve Shopify's design system

## Requirements

- 5+ years of product design experience
- Strong portfolio showing end-to-end product work
- Proficiency in Figma and prototyping tools
- Experience working in cross-functional agile teams
